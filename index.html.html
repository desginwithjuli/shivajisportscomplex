About Us

Learn about our mission to promote fitness and sports excellence.

Vipin Dev

Founder, Shiva Ji Sports Complex

Vipin Dev, Founder of Shiva Ji Sports Complex, has dedicated his life to promoting fitness, youth engagement, and holistic sports education. Under his vision, this center offers a wide range of sports and wellness programs for all age groups.

With over 15 years of experience in sports management and coaching, Vipin has created a facility that combines traditional values with modern training techniques, ensuring that every participant receives the best possible guidance in their chosen activity.
